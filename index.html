I'm a cis male. The closet I'm coming out of doesn't have a rainbow on the door, it has a capital ess and a lowercase zee. Sz.


That's what I call Schizophrenia, and I pronounce it skizz. Butwait. Before you let that label take you places, let me say this.


Sz does not mean psycho. Psychopathic is a whole different ballgame. Sz (usually) means suffering (episodes of) psychosis.


What's psychosis? Well, an episode usually entails hallucinations, delusions, and mood changes. The more mood-affecting, the more likely it's Schizoaffective Disorder, not Sz. Or it could be Bipolar with Psychosis. It's, like, a spectrum.


So but yeah, I got it. Had it for over a dozen years now. For me it was a big year-long episode at onset, and rare relapse-type events since.


Delusions follow on the heels of hallucinations. When you hear somebody who isn't there saying something like "I am not you," it sets you off thinking who it might be, and that's where delusions kick in.
